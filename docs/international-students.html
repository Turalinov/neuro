International students

Welcome international students to the Neuro Business School! We offer unique learning opportunities for students worldwide to study neuroscience in the beautiful city of Barcelona.

Benefits and support for NBS students

Help with accommodation
NBS provides assistance to students in finding suitable accommodation options
Help with student visa
International students often require assistance with the student visa application process. NBS seems to offer support in this regard, helping students navigate the visa application process, providing necessary documentation, and ensuring compliance with immigration requirements
Assistance for the whole period of studying for our students:
We offer comprehensive support throughout your studying journey at our Neuro business school in a new country. Our team will communicate with you regularly to guide and assist with training or adaptation challenges. We will also arrange meetings with your mentors for progress discussions and addressing any concerns or questions you may have.
Student cards and discounts
NBS provides student identification cards, which can provide access to various benefits and discounts. These cards can be used for discounts on events, shopping, and various services, making student life more affordable. At the same time, we offer extracurricular activities to help you make new friends and enjoy your studies. We can also organise cultural trips to explore the region.

In our Neuro business school, we will make every effort to make your year of study complete, interesting and unforgettable.

We care about every student, and your success and comfort come first for us!
