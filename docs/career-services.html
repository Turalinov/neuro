Career Services

  • We help our students achieve success in the labor market. Our team of professional consultants and mentors is ready to help you every step of the way to a successful career.

    At NBS, we recognise that searching for a job can be a difficult and stressful process. That's why we offer career support to help you get the most out of your career.

  • One of our main services is the preparation and execution of resumes. We'll help you highlight your skills and accomplishments to make your resume stand out to employers. Our consultants also have extensive experience in preparing resumes for various fields of activity, so you can be sure of receiving professional assistance.

    In addition, we offer interview preparation that includes practice lessons, interview simulations, and a detailed review of your preparation. Our consultants share the secrets to a successful interview and will help you confidently and effectively present your skills and experience.

  • We also provide personalized career counseling to help you identify your career goals and develop a personalized strategy for success. Our consultants will work with you to develop the skills you need and provide you with valuable advice and information about opportunities in the job market.

    We are committed to providing comprehensive support to our students, helping you turn your efforts into concrete results. No matter where you are on your career journey, our career services are a trusted resource you can count on for help.

    Don't lose the opportunity to receive quality assistance in your career development. Contact us today and let's achieve your career goals together!
