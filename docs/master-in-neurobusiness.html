    Program Objectives

    Neurobusiness is an innovative business approach that applies the principles of neuroscience to understand and improve management, sales, and overall business performance. It explores how our brain affects our behavior, both within organizations and as salespeople, marketers, and consumers. These practical sessions focus on key areas such as marketing, sales, leadership, and management.

    Specifics

    Neurobusiness is not just a concept; it's a transformative discipline that harnesses the insights from neuroscience to enhance decision-making, leadership, and overall business performance. It's the art and science of applying brain-based principles to optimise various facets of corporate operations.

Explore latest technologies at the class:

NEURO Business School focuses on integrating technology and innovation into business education. We aim to stay ahead in the dynamic field of neuromarketing. Through partnerships with leading companies, we provide unique learning experiences using cutting-edge technologies. Our curriculum and research center around Eyetracking and Electroencephalography (EEG).

  • What is Eyetracking?

    Eyetracking is a sophisticated technology that monitors and records eye movements and gaze points. It enables us to understand precisely where a person's eyes focus and how they move across a visual stimulus, such as an advertisement or a webpage.

  • What is EEG?

    Electroencephalography (EEG) is a non-invasive technique that records electrical activity in the brain. It measures brainwave patterns and provides insights into cognitive processes, emotions, and reactions.
